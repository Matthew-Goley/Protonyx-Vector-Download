Capabilities

Built for clarity, not clutter.

📊

Modular Dashboard

Drag-and-drop widget system. See only what matters — allocation breakdowns, performance charts, risk metrics — arranged your way.

⚡

Real-Time Analytics

Live portfolio valuation and performance tracking. Instant feedback on how your positions are moving throughout the day.

💾

Persistent Tracking

Your portfolio data saves locally between sessions. Pick up exactly where you left off without re-entering positions.

Local & Private

Everything runs on your machine. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data collection. Your portfolio stays yours.

Answer-Driven

Ask questions about your portfolio and get direct, quantitative answers. No digging through charts to find the signal.

Native Desktop

Fast, lightweight Windows app. No browser tabs, no Electron bloat — just a clean tool that opens and works.
